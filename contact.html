Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons, response times may extend to 3 business days. For urgent matters, please call our customer service line during business hours for immediate assistance.

Do you offer beekeeping workshops or training?

Yes! We offer monthly beginner beekeeping workshops at our headquarters and quarterly advanced courses. Check our Events page for the current schedule. We also provide private consultations for businesses or community groups interested in urban beekeeping.

Can I visit your urban apiaries?

For safety and security reasons, most of our rooftop apiaries are not open to the public. However, we do offer guided tours of our educational apiary on select weekends. Please contact us for availability and scheduling.

What's the best way to contact you about hosting a hive?

Please use our contact form and select "Partnership Opportunities" as the subject, or email hives@homiehoney.com directly. Include details about your location (address, rooftop/balcony specs) and we'll schedule a consultation to assess feasibility.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale honey purchases?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for orders of 12+ jars of the same honey variety. Please contact our sales team at sales@homiehoney.com or call ext. 2 for pricing and availability. Minimum order quantities may apply depending on season.
